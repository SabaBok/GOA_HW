Cambridge


Acceptance Rate:
21%


Tuition: $59K

Founded in 1209, the University of Cambridge in England is one of the oldest and most prestigious universities in the world. It was established by scholars leaving Oxford due to disputes and has since built a reputation for academic rigor and groundbreaking research. Cambridge’s commitment to fostering intellectual curiosity is evident in its unique collegiate system, with 31 autonomous colleges. The university’s motto, "Hinc lucem et pocula sacra" (From here, light and sacred draughts), reflects its dedication to both knowledge and enlightenment.
