67%


Tuition: $4.5K

Founded in 1922, Georgian Technical University (GTU) in Tbilisi, Georgia, is one of the country's leading institutions for engineering, technology, and applied sciences. It was established to meet the growing demand for technical education in Georgia and has since become a key player in the development of the country's industrial and technological sectors. GTU offers a wide range of programs in fields such as engineering, computer science, architecture, and management, with a focus on practical skills and innovation. The university is committed to preparing students for the challenges of a rapidly evolving global economy.
