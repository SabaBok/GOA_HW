Acceptance Rate:
16.8%


Tuition: $14,000

Founded in 1868, the University of California, Berkeley (UC Berkeley) is one of the most prestigious and influential public universities in the world. Located in Berkeley, California, UC Berkeley has been at the forefront of scientific discovery, social justice movements, and technological innovation. UC Berkeley is known for its diverse academic programs, commitment to research, and its role in shaping public policy and social change. With a focus on both undergraduate and graduate education.

UC Berkeley is the perfect mix of intellectual rigor and cultural diversity. The professors are experts in their fields, and the opportunities for interdisciplinary studies are vast. The campus is buzzing with energy, and you’re always encouraged to think critically about the world.
