Tuition: $200,000

Founded in 1861, the Massachusetts Institute of Technology (MIT) in Cambridge, Massachusetts, is a global leader in science, engineering, and technology. Established during the Industrial Revolution, MIT was designed to meet the nation’s growing demand for advanced technical education. MIT’s mission is to advance knowledge and educate students in ways that serve the world. Known for fostering innovation, it combines rigorous academics with hands-on problem-solving to address real-world challenges. Its motto, “Mens et Manus” (Mind and Hand), reflects the integration of theoretical learning with practical application.
