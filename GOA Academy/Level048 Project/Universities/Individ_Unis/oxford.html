The University of Oxford, established by 1096, is the oldest university in the English-speaking world. Known for its academic excellence, it comprises 39 colleges offering personalized education and advancing global knowledge. Its motto, "Dominus Illuminatio Mea" (The Lord is My Light), reflects its dedication to enlightenment and truth. Oxford has shaped history, producing 27 British Prime Ministers, 72 Nobel laureates, and leaders in diverse fields. From historic landmarks like the Bodleian Library to groundbreaking research, Oxford seamlessly blends tradition with innovation.
