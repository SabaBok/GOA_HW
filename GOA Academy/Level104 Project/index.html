Norma

Norma is a modern employment platform designed to streamline the connection between job seekers and business owners. Inspired by platforms like Indeed, Norma expands on the traditional job board model by offering a dual-profile system—empowering both applicants and employers to take an active role in the hiring process.

At its core, Norma is more than just a job listing site. It allows job seekers to create comprehensive profiles that showcase their skills, experience, and professional interests. These profiles are discoverable by business owners, who can browse, search, and invite suitable candidates directly—eliminating the wait for applications. Likewise, employers can build company profiles, post job openings, and specify exactly the type of talent they’re looking for. Instead of passively posting jobs and waiting for applicants, Norma enables a proactive approach: business owners can explore the talent pool and reach out to candidates who match their needs.
