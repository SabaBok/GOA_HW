Founded in 1754, Columbia University in New York City is one of the oldest Ivy League institutions and a leading center of academic excellence. Originally known as King's College, it was established under a royal charter and has since become a beacon of knowledge, research, and societal impact. Columbia is renowned for its rigorous academic programs, innovative research, and diverse global community. The university offers a wide range of programs through its Colleges, Graduate Schools, and Research Institutes, focusing on interdisciplinary learning and societal contributions.
