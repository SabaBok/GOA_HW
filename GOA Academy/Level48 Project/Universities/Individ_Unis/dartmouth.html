Dartmouth


Acceptance Rate:
6.2%


Tuition: $60,000

Founded in 1769, Dartmouth College in Hanover, New Hampshire, is one of the oldest Ivy League institutions. Originally established to educate Native American youth, Dartmouth has since evolved into a prestigious institution known for its strong focus on undergraduate education and commitment to fostering leadership, critical thinking, and service. Dartmouth offers a unique liberal arts experience, with a tight-knit community that fosters close relationships between students and faculty. The college is known for its outdoor activities, strong academic programs, and emphasis on undergraduate research.
