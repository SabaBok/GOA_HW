Switzerland, a landlocked country in the heart of Europe, is famed for its stunning alpine scenery, high quality of life, and multicultural society. Known for its neutrality, Switzerland has remained a peaceful, stable nation and is home to many international organizations, including the Red Cross and the UN offices in Geneva. The country's breathtaking landscapes, including the Swiss Alps and pristine lakes, attract tourists year-round for skiing, hiking, and sightseeing. Switzerland is also celebrated for its precision engineering, luxury watches, and world-class chocolates, making it a blend of natural beauty, cultural heritage, and modern sophistication.
