Aston Martin DBS

Specs
  • Engine: 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12
  • Horsepower: 725 hp
  • Torque: 900 Nm
  • Transmission: 8-speed automatic
  • 0-100 km/h: ~3.4 seconds
  • Top Speed: 340 km/h
  • Weight: ~1,693 kg

    • The Aston Martin DBS, a flagship grand tourer from the renowned British automaker, embodies luxury, performance, and sophistication. Introduced in 2007 and revitalized in 2018, the DBS showcases a perfect blend of timeless design and cutting-edge technology. Powered by a formidable 5.2-liter twin-turbocharged V12 engine, the DBS delivers exhilarating performance, producing up to 725 horsepower. With such power, the DBS can accelerate from 0 to 100 km/h in just 3.4 seconds, making it one of the fastest Aston Martins to date. The DBS features an advanced 8-speed automatic transmission, ensuring smooth and rapid gear changes. The all-wheel-drive system provides excellent traction, enhancing the driving experience whether on winding roads or straight highways. Its lightweight aluminum structure and carbon fiber components contribute to its agile handling and overall performance. The design of the DBS is as striking as its performance. With aggressive lines, a distinctive front grille, and a luxurious interior filled with premium materials, the DBS offers an unparalleled driving experience. The cabin is equipped with the latest technology, including a state-of-the-art infotainment system, ensuring that both comfort and connectivity are prioritized. Aston Martin's DBS is not just a car; it's a statement of elegance and power, appealing to those who seek performance without sacrificing luxury.
